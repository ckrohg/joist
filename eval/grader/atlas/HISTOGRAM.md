## Detector limits (recorded, not hidden)

- **Dynamic behaviors are invisible in static capture trees**: marquee, carousel, accordion-collapse,
  and modal all classify as `dyn:static` (only `sticky` and `tabs` are structurally detectable).
  Their atlas cells are seeded from the motion memories/vj evidence with `histogramCount: 0`.
- **clerk contributes band-only keys** (no typed tree in `/tmp/local-fidelity/cap`): 134 bands across
  4 widths collapse onto a handful of `block-stack|band-only|…` keys. Structure inside the bands is
  uncounted — undercounts composites, never inflates them.
- **card-grid (3) and logo-band (0) under-detect**: many real grids appear as `flex-row|containers-only`
  or `grid-*|containers-only` whose children are wrapper containers; the alias rules require direct
  card/logo evidence. The raw grid/flex-row key-triples (rows 8, 20, 26…) carry the counts instead.
- **hero-stack alias is the most-common-alias label on broad keys** — e.g. row 8 shows 12 hero matches
  out of 60 occurrences of that key; the JSON keeps exact per-alias counts.
- **No `divider` kind exists in the capture vocabulary** — divider/rule constructs are seeded from the
  capability matrix without histogram counts.
