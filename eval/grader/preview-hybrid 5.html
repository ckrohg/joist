Book of the week

Entrepreneurship starts with ideas.

The Fabric of Civilization: How Textiles Made the World

Virginia Postrel

Textiles are the foundational technology of modern civilization. Postrel traces how the scarcity of thread drove the Industrial Revolution, how weaving gave us the origins of binary code, and how the textile trade built the institutions of banking and global commerce we know today. The book argues that most of this progress came not from celebrated inventors but from incremental process improvements by anonymous workers.
